How You Train

Your Pace. Your Schedule.

Not everyone can take two weeks off work. That's fine — we built both options from the ground up.

⚡ Accelerated Track
Full-time · Intensive · Fastest path to rated

This is aviation boot camp — in the best way. You fly every day, study every evening, and build the kind of mental muscle memory that comes only from total immersion. Your checkride date is locked in before you even arrive, so there's a hard target on the horizon.

Best for pilots who want to complete a rating in the shortest possible time. Come ready to work.
🗓 Flexible Track
Part-time · Self-paced · Fits your life

Train on weekends, evenings, or a few days a week. You set the tempo and we keep your training consistent and progressive. No pressure to rush — just steady, quality progress toward your certificate.

Best for working professionals, students, or anyone who wants to train without putting life on hold.
